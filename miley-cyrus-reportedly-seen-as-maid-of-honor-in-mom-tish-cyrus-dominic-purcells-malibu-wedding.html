Miley Cyrus Reportedly Seen as Maid of Honor in Mom Tish Cyrus, Dominic Purcells Malibu Wedding

Reportedly, Tish Cyrus recently married Prison Break Dominic Purcell, and her daughter Miley Cyrus offered full support. The “Wrecking Ball” hitmaker attended her mother’s wedding in Malibu, California and served as the lovely bride’s maid of honor.

Miley Cyrus Attends Tish Cyrus’s Intimate Wedding

Clearly emotional and happy for Tish, Miley was wearing an ice blue one-shoulder dress during the ceremony. She was by her mom’s side when she walked down the aisle, visibly giving her blessing to Tish and Purcell’s romance.

As per the snaps obtained by Just Jared, Tish looked ethereal in a strapless lace bridal gown with a long tulle veil. Meanwhile, Purcell sported a white dress shirt in rolled up sleeves and black trousers.

Miley’s older siblings, Trace Cyrus and Brandi Cyrus, were also spotted in the intimate ceremony.

Tish went Instagram official with Purcell in November 2022, sharing a photograph of herself cuddling with the actor. She then revealed her engagement to Purcell on Instagram last April. Then, she shared a pair of photos flaunting her beautiful diamond ring.

“A thousand times…. YES @dominicpurcell,” she wrote.

Tish and her former husband Billy Ray Cyrus officially separated in 2022 after 28 years of marriage. Before finally enforcing their divorce, the two called it quits twice — in 2010 and 2013.

She Unfollowed Her Father on Instagram

While it’s obvious that Miley is absolutely okay with her mom marrying another guy, her feeling is not the same on her father’s side. Sources told that Miley and her father are “no longer on speaking terms.”

After the divorce, Miley unfollowed her dad on Instagram. This incident, though, did not prove to be an accident as Billy Ray eventually unfollowed his daughter as a response.

As per reports, this family feud is highly related to Billy Ray’s shocking engagement with Australian singer Firerose. Allegedly, Miley does not approve of the relationship primarily because of the age of her father’s fiancée. At present, it’s not clear if Miley and Billy Ray have reconciled. Nonetheless, it’s fair to think that the tension is still high between Cyrus’s family members.
