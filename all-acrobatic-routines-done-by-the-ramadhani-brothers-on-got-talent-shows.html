All Acrobatic Routines Done By The Ramadhani Brothers on 'Got Talent' Shows

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Trending

Talent Recap Thumbnail

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The Ramadhani Brothers, consisting of the acrobatic duo Fadhili Ramadhani and Ibrahim Jobu from Tanzania, have wowed audiences around the world with their incredible strength and balance routines on got talent competitions. Their impressive feats of agility and athleticism have earned them a spot on America’s Got Talent: Fantasy League as one of the returning 40 acts.

Ramadhani Brothers All Got Talent Performances

Australia’s Got Talent 2022

The duo’s journey began on the 2022 season of Australia’s Got Talent, where they amazed the judges and viewers with their acrobatic yoga and adagio acro moves, even getting the Golden Buzzer from Kate Ritchie to advance directly to the semi-finals.

France’s Got Talent 2022

On France’s Got Talent 2022, the brothers once again stunned with their technically difficult act, using a removable cylinder prop to perform head-to-head balances while walking upstairs backward. Though they nearly fell at the end, the French judges praised them for risk-taking and surpassing themselves.

Romania’s Got Talent Season 13

The Ramadhani Brothers then competed on season 13 of Romania’s Got Talent in 2023, making it all the way to the semi-finals.

Spain’s Got Talent: All Stars 2023

They then took part in Spain’s Got Talent All-Stars, an international competition featuring winners, finalists, and viral talents from Got Talents around the world. They took their balancing routine to the next level by stepping on a ladder platform instead of the usual stairs. The brothers incorporated strength and yoga moves as well, which brought them to the finale where the audience votes determined the winner.

Czechoslovakia’s Got Talent 2023

On Czechoslovakia’s Got Talent 2023, the Tanzanian acrobats once again got the Golden Buzzer from judge Diana Morova to advance directly to the final. They also reached the final later in 2023 on the tenth season of the Italian competition series Italy’s Tu Si Que Vales.

America’s Got Talent Season 18

Most notably, The Ramadhani Brothers were semi-finalists on season 18 of America’s Got Talent, getting yes votes from all four judges during their audition. In the semi-finals, they performed a highly dangerous hand and head balancing act which earned them standing ovations from the judges, advancing them to the finals.

Their pre-recorded finals routine was just as daring and difficult, set to “Eye of the Tiger”, again wowing the judges. In the finale, they collaborated with kid dance crew Chibi Unity, making the top five before finishing in fifth place.
