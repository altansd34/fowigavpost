'American Idol' Star Benson Boone Sold Out His USA Tour Tickets In Less Than a Minute

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Photo by: Lloyd Bishop/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In less than a minute, American Idol star Benson Boone was able to sell out all his latest USA tour. The multi-instrumentalist will be doing three shows in Salt Lake City, Utah, following fan requests.

Benson Boone’s Tour is Sold Out

On October 26, Benson Boone shared to Instagram that his four stop tour is completely sold out. Superfans were quick to jump on the ticket sale, since the shows sold out before October 28’s general sale could even begin. Fans of the singer are hoping he will extend the tour and add more dates as it gets closer.

In the same post, the singer announced that he will be adding another show in Utah for his other fans who were unable to buy tickets. Apparently, even Boone’s mom could not purchase tickets due to the intense competition of fans.

The second wave of tickets quickly sold out as well, prompting Boone to ask if fans will be willing for a third show.

“For people in Utah, I might have to do a third show, because literally you sold out these two shows in less than 10 seconds,” he said in his Instagram story. “So, should I post a third show?”

Finally, the American Idol star decided to book another Utah show. This made some fans relieved and left others begging for a fourth show. Boone’s USA tour will kick off on December 1 in Salt Lake City and will end on December 9 in Santa Ana California.

He Will Be Releasing New Music

For quite some time, Boone has been teasing new music on Instagram and TikTok. The singer created “Before You” and describes it as a wedding song. Just recently, Boone revealed the bridge of the song on YouTube shorts.

“Before You” will officially be released on October 28. For now, fans can pre-save it on Spotify and Deezer and pre-add on Apple Music. Surely, fans are excited for this romantic song. Earlier this year, Boone told fans that he’s been working on some new songs, so many expect that he has more releases on the rise.

This past July, Boone released his most recent EP, Walk Me Home… The release featured his hit songs “GHOST TOWN,” “In The Stars,” “NIGHTS LIKE THESE,” and “ROOM FOR 2.” Fans are still loving this release, two months later.
