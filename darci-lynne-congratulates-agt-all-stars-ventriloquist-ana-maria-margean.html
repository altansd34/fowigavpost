America’s Got Talent winner Darci Lynne Farmer took to Instagram this week to congratulate fellow young ventriloquist Ana-Maria Mărgean on making it to the AGT: All-Stars Finals. Ana-Maria earned the Superfan vote during Monday’s new episode.

Darci Lynne Farmer Congratulates Ana-Maria Mărgean

On Monday night, 13-year-old Romania’s Got Talent winner Ana-Maria Mărgean performed an incredible ventriloquism routine in which she and her puppet Waldo sang “You Don’t Own Me.” The judges were blown away, and the Superfans voted her through to the Finals.

AGT Season 12 winner Darci Lynne Farmer took to Instagram Tuesday to share her excitement over Ana-Maria’s success. She shared AGT‘s Instagram post about Ana-Maria and wrote, “CONGRATULATIONS superstar!!!!” Farmer added, “We are rooting for you!!!” Farmer also commented on the show’s post, sharing three clapping hands emojis and writing, “woohoo!!!”

Season 2 winner Terry Fator also reacted to Ana-Maria’s performance after she shared that he inspired her to take up ventriloquism. As he wrote on Instagram, “Wow, so proud to have inspired such a talented young ventriloquist.”

Ana-Maria responded to Fator’s post, writing, “Thank you so much! This means so much to me!” She also reposted Farmer’s comments, responding with a heart emoji.

Farmer Competed on AGT: The Champions

Farmer herself is no stranger to a competition like All-Stars. In 2019, she competed on the first season of AGT: The Champions, which also featured acts from Got Talent shows around the world competing for the ultimate title.

Although Farmer was initially eliminated in the Preliminaries, she ended up returning as a wildcard act in the Champions Finals, and she finished the competition in second place behind magician Shin Lim.

Similarly, Fator competed earlier this season on AGT: All-Stars. However, he was unfortunately eliminated after his first performance. Fator got fans talking earlier this month when he suggested that he’ll be back for the finale. Will he be performing with Ana-Maria? We’ll have to wait and see.
