&quot;7 Feet 9 inches&quot;: Tall Man Appears in Public, People Surround Him Like Celebrity in Viral Video

  • A very tall man appeared in public, and he instantly turned into a celebrity because people marvelled at his height
  • Many people gathered around him, trying to touch and take pictures with him to keep as a memory of meeting him

A TikTok video shows a tall man who measures a whopping 7 feet 9 inches in height.

The man named Malith Dak Deng instantly became a celebrity as many people came to meet him when he appeared in public.

In the video posted by @kafuki_jada, the man was surrounded by a large crowd of admirers who came to take photos with him.
