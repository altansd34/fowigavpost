Hottest Strip Tease Acts on 'Got Talent' Worldwide

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Talent Recap

Reading Time: 3 minutes

While awkward and exasperating sometimes, Got Talent competitions are not bereft of strip tease performances throughout the years. These acts may have not always been the most pleasing in sight, but there’s no denying that they make the show more fun, dynamic, and entertaining.

Hottest Strip Tease Acts on Got Talent Worldwide

1. Lorna Bliss, Britain’s Got Talent 2011

Lorna Bliss graced Britain’s Got Talent season five as a Britney Spears impersonator. Prior to her audition, Bliss stated that she “basically dedicated [her] whole being to being Britney.” She first appeared onstage, performing “Toxic” by Britney Spears and wearing the same alluring outfit that the singer wore.

2. The Forbidden, Britain’s Got Talent 2023

Also known as The Forbidden Circus of Men, The Forbidden is a burlesque group who joined season 16 of BGT. The group originally introduced themselves as a magic act, eventually performing a strip tease act to “Pony” by Ginuwine.

3. Wild Girls, Ukraine’s Got Talent

Wild Girls entered Ukraine’s Got Talent as a hot female duo in pink. During their audition, they performed a sexy and steamy acrobatic routine. Clearly, male judges and the audience are amused by their performance.

4. Fabia Cerra, Britain’s Got Talent 2009

Aged 35 at the time of her audition, Fabia Cerra was a burlesque dancer from season three of BGT. She kicked off her audition wearing a top-hat and suit which were instantly removed. With her bold act, the show was forced to censor the undressed parts of her body.

5. Brandon Coprich, America’s Got Talent 2019

Brandon Coprich introduced himself as a stripper and a flautist back in season 14 of AGT. He began his audition by playing Edvard Grieg’s “Morning Mood” using the flute. Then, surprisingly, he removed his clothes and played Ginuwine’s “Pony” while doing vulgar dance moves.

6. Beatrix Von Bourbon, Britain’s Got Talent 2012

Hailing from London, Beatrix Von Bourbon is a professional burlesque dancer from season three of BGT. Her audition consists of doing a briefcase stunt before taking off her jacket and stripping in front of the crowd.

7. Marvin Achi, America’s Got Talent 2022

Season 17 of AGT featured chemical engineer Marvin Achi. During his audition, he pretended to work on an experiment, but, chemicals spilled on his shirt. Soon after, he stripped off his lab coat and shirt and proceeded to show off his muscles and pecs.

8. Nicolae Florin Lupsor, Spain’s Got Talent 2019

Nicolae Florin Lupsor was a strip tease act from Got Talent España. During his act, he pretended to play the piano, wearing a complete set of men’s suit. He then surprised the audience when he gradually removed each piece of the suit, leaving his blue undies for the audience to see.

9. Dorothy Williams, America’s Got Talent 2016

Dorothy Williams was a burlesque dancer from season 11 of America’s Got Talent. To start off, she briefly sang “Let Me Entertain You” from Gypsy. Then, she danced and stripped what she was wearing to David Rose’s “The Stripper.”

10. Scarlet Cuffs, Britain’s Got Talent 2013

Scarlet Cuffs was a solo dancer from season seven of Britain’s Got Talent. During her audition, she sang “The Bare Necessities” from Disney’s The Jungle Book. Shockingly, she unzipped her gold dress stripped naked.
