There has been one very noticeable thing that Khloe Kardashian has included in every single one of her Instagram pictures recently. Have you seen that massive diamond ring that is always on her ring finger? The ring is seriously hard to miss in her latest post where fans are convinced the 36-year-old is engaged.

Is Khloe Kardashian Engaged?

Khloe showed off her gorgeous pastel manicure in an up-close picture on Instagram. She captioned the photo with a serious of rainbow heart emojis. But all eyes are on that shiny diamond ring. “Tell us you’re engaged without telling us you’re engaged,” one fan wrote in the comments section.

“This HAS to be an engagement ring” another fan pointed out.

“This ring has had more photoshoots than vogue and no official announcement” added another. Other’s on Twitter also wondered if the Good American founder is engaged.

One fan directly replied to Khloe’s tweet asking her if she is engaged.

Khloe’s baby daddy Tristan Thompson’s comment on the post fueled engagement rumors even more. Tristan commented “👀👀😍😍💎💎” which clearly points out the ring through the use of emojis.

During an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Ellen asked Kris Jenner about Khloe’s ring. “That’s a good ring. I think she just loves jewelry. She loves a good, you know, ring!” Kris said. “You should ask Khloe that.” 

What Is The Status Of Khloe And Tristan’s Relationship?

During the current season of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Khloe and Tristan announced their plans to have another baby through IVF. On the show, the pair were not having a physical relationship at the time but instead co-parenting their 2-year-old daughter True. The show was filmed over the course of a few months and Khloe did say at the time that she and the NBA star were “figuring things out.”

Now based on recent Instagram posts and comments, it seems pretty clear that they are once again a couple. Their relationship was in shambles back in 2019 when Tristan kissed Kylie Jenner’s bestie Jordyn Woods at a party. He has also been accused of infidelity multiple times in the past.

“I’m so grateful that I decided to make embryos. I’m 36, but as young as I am, what if I was 40 and then my eggs aren’t as healthy, so then I’d have to do IVF again to make embryos, and we realized my eggs aren’t strong enough to be frozen,” she said in an interview. “They should be mixed immediately with sperm to make embryos.”

Ellen asked Kris how she feels about Khloe and Tristan trying to have another baby. “I mean, I think it’s so great that they want to do that again and have a sibling for True, because there’s nothing better than a big family and brothers and sisters, and that’s just my heart,” she said. “So I’m really happy. I just want them to be happy. And True will be so excited.”
