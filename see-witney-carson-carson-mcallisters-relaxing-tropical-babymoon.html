See Witney Carson, Carson McAllister's Relaxing Tropical Babymoon

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Witney Carson

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Ahead of their second baby’s arrival, Dancing With The Stars pro Witney Carson and husband Carson McAllister enjoyed a luxurious beach trip. According to the dancer, the said babymoon is their way to reconnect before exploring a new chapter with two kids.

Witney Carson, Carson McAllister Spent a Relaxing Babymoon

Talking to Us Weekly, Carson shared how they spent their days at Sandals Grande St. Lucian Resort. Staying in the luxurious Rondoval Butler Suite, the married couple relaxed in the pool and jacuzzi of the area, thus having a well-served babymoon.

“We’ve loved swinging on the bed swing at the beach and swimming in the ocean, then coming back to the room to lay out by our private pool,” she said.

As stated by the DWTS season 19 champion, it’s been an important part of her relationship with the mechanical engineer to have a solo trip once a year. Since they’ve become parents, the pair has always made sure to have a love birds-exclusive bonding moment.

“For the past two years, we’ve loved our experience at Sandals!” she said. “We both think it’s crucial to make time for each other, especially when life gets crazy.”

For those who may not know, Carson and McAllister were high school sweethearts before they became husband and wife. They share 2-year-old son Kevin Leo, who they welcomed after tying the know back in January 2016.

She Gives Vacation Updates Through a Series of Instagram Snaps

On Instagram, Carson was generous in providing adorable snaps of their babymoon in Sta. Lucia. For one, she uploaded a slideshow of bikini photos, confidently posing with the ocean as her background while showing off her baby bump.

“Growing a human is a cause for celebration,” she wrote in the caption.

Soon after this post, she shared pictures of her and McAllister while on a date. She then posted more bikini photos to serve as a countdown of the arrival of their baby. In her caption, Carson stated that “extra pounds just means extra love,” marking her 31 weeks of pregnancy.

Carson and McAllister evidently enjoyed the trip, saying that they’ve had a laid-back vacation this time. They did some golf at the Cap Estate Golf and Country Club, eat pizza, and listen to music.

“Last year we did lots of excursions and more activities, and this time around we relaxed by the beach and pool! Both trips were so amazing in different ways,” she said.
