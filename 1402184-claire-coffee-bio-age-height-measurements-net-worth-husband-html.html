Education

She attended the Santa Catalina School for girls and has a degree in Theatre from Northwestern University School of Communication.

She also trained at The Groundlings, Upright Citizens Brigade, and Improv Olympic.

Career

She began performing on stage at age 5 in The Mountain Play Theater Company's production of The King and I in Mill Valley, CA. She continued acting extensively on stage before moving to Los Angeles after college.

She had a recurring role on The West Wing as Cassie Tatum in 2003. From 2007 to 2009, Coffee played nurse Nadine Crowell in 170 episodes of the TV series General Hospital.

From 2011 to 2012, she played attorney Janie Ross on the TNT series Franklin & Bash. She played Hexenbiest Adalind Schade for six seasons and 89 episodes of the series Grimm.
