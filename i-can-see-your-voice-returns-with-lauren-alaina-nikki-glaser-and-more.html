I Can See Your Voice returns for Season 3 on Wednesday night, and the premiere episode will feature a few famous faces. That includes American Idol alum Lauren Alaina and The Masked Singer contestant Nikki Glaser.

I Can See Your Voice Returns with New Celebrity Detectives

The premise of I Can See Your Voice is that contestants are tasked with determining whether people are good or bad singers based on only on their appearance and clues about them. There to help them is a panel of celebrity guest detectives.

Wednesday’s new episode will feature regular detectives Adrienne Bailon-Houghton and Cheryl Hines, as well as host Ken Jeong. Joining them will be American Idol Season 10 runner-up Lauren Alaina and comedian Nikki Glaser, who appeared on The Masked Singer Season 8 as Snowstorm.

The episode will also feature DeRay Davis, a comedian known for his role in the Barbershop movies. The celebrity detectives will be on hand to try and determine whether people are good or bad singers. In a preview clip from the premiere, a woman lip syncs to a cover of  “Just Give Me a Reason” by Pink.

Who Else Is Featured This Season?

Celebrity guest detectives have also been announced for later in the season. They include Jimmie Allen, Gavin DeGraw, Kate Flannery, Todrick Hall, Cheyenne Jackson, Penn Jillette, Chris Kirkpatrick, NeNe Leakes, Frankie Muniz, Dionne Warwick, Michelle Williams, Carnie Wilson, and more.

This season will also feature themed episodes such as Queen Night, Motown Night, and Divas Night. This is similar to The Masked Singer, which has incorporated themes into its various episodes in recent seasons.

Are you excited about the new season? Let us know in the comments below. Be sure to tune in to the premiere of I Can See Your Voice Season 3 Wednesday night at 8/7c on FOX.
