9 Celebrities That Changed So Much, Their Fans Dont Even Recognize Them

August 2024 · 3 minute read

A lot of women love experimenting with their appearance. But sometimes, it’s not so easy to change your regular look, even if you really want to. If you’ve been considering a change in your style, check out how some of these stars did it.

We at Bright Side really like how some celebrities handle their change of style. But honestly, we think that anything looks good on them.

1. Lucy Liu

When fans saw Lucy Liu as a blonde, they were really surprised. The actress appeared with new hair in public in 2018. She was inspired to do this by her role in Stage Mother that was released in 2020.

After that, she returned to her dark hair color, but she had her long hair cut shorter.

2. Lady Gaga

100% better look for her. She actually looks pretty now. I thought her before look was not attractive at all.

In the past, the edgy Lady Gaga chose very bright clothes and overall looks for her everyday life. But now, she shares more and more no-makeup photos. One of the most recent changes she’s posted on Instagram is her look donning more natural makeup without such bright blonde hair. She’s still edgy in her public event looks though.

3. Keanu Reeves

A very unusual look of the actor was captured by photographers on the set of a film. The Keanu that we’re used to seeing with longer hair was completely bald.

It was probably because he started working on a new Matrix film that is supposed to be released in December of 2021.

4. Charlize Theron

Love her shorter hair. Very pretty lady.

Charlize Theron looks great with different hairstyles and colors.

So in 2019, she replaced her blonde hair with a short, dark hairstyle. But in 2020, Charlize returned to the more typical blonde hue but cut it even shorter.

5. Zoë Kravitz

At the very end of 2019, the actress got rid of her long dreadlocks. According to some rumors, she had her hair cut short for a role in a new Batman film. In any case, Zoë loved the new style so much that it doesn’t look like she’s going to change it anytime soon.

6. Kristen Stewart

For several years, Kristen Stewart has had quite short hair. In 2019, she had her hair cut almost completely off for a role, and such an appearance really reflects the temperament of her character.

7. Adele

The entire world has been watching Adele’s weight loss journey for more than a year now. Adele really wanted to lose weight after divorcing her husband. She’s lost quite a few pounds and is experimenting with clothes and hairstyles that she shares with fans online.

8. Jessica Simpson

Jessica Simpson managed to lose 100 lb. Before her second pregnancy and during the time after it, she’s gained some extra weight, and then she wanted to get back in shape. She says she managed to achieve such stunning results with the right food and athletic activity.

9. Cristiano Ronaldo

When the leader of Juventus, Cristiano Ronaldo, posted his new photo online, fans found out that he had cut his hair short while he was ill. Fans had mixed reactions to his new image.

Do you like the new looks of these stars? Maybe some other celebrities changed their looks and we forgot to mention them in our compilation. Let us know down below!
