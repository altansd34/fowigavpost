'RuPaul's Drag Race' Returns with an Unforgettable 'All Stars 8' Premiere

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM More Shows RuPaul's Drag Race

RuPaul's Drag Race All Stars/Paramount+

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Get ready to sashay, shantay, and slay because RuPaul’s Drag Race is back with a bang, and this time it’s All Stars season eight! The highly anticipated season kicked off with a captivating 15-minute premiere that left fans on the edge of their seats. With a thrilling twist and a dazzling display of talent, this sneak peek sets the stage for an exhilarating journey into the world of fierce competition, unparalleled glamour, and unforgettable performances.

RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars Released First 15 Minutes of The New Season

The short premiere opens up by introducing a cast that exudes fierce determination and a burning desire to snatch the crown. RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars 8 promises to be a season like no other. The journey is set to be filled with emotional highs and lows, unexpected twists, and unparalleled talent.

The show brings together a powerhouse lineup of queens ready to slay the competition. Season eight’s Naysha Lopez returns, exuding beauty and charisma, while Season two’s Jessica Wild brings her comedic prowess and captivating performances. Other returning queens include Monica Beverly Hillz from season five, Alexis Michelle from season nine, and Lala Ri from season 13 among others.

Season twelve’s fan-favorite Heidi N Closet, season seven’s beloved Mrs. Kasha Davis, the fierce Kahanna Montrese from season eleven, and the charismatic Kandy Muse from season thirteen are all set to return to the competition. Joining them are the talented Darienne Lake from season six, the quirky and delightful Jaymes Mansfield from season nine, and the unforgettable Jimbo from Canada’s Drag Race vs The World.

From the electrifying lip-sync battles to the heartwarming moments of vulnerability, viewers will witness the queens’ personal growth, resilience, and unwavering commitment to their craft. These extraordinary queens compete for a chance to secure their place in the Drag Race Hall of Fame and claim the coveted $200,000 cash prize.

RuPaul Charles Announces All New Additions to The Show

In this electrifying opening act, the iconic Mama Ru enters the Werk Room, exuding an air of anticipation and mystery. As he addresses the 12 returning queens, it then becomes clear that something extraordinary awaits them.

RuPaul wastes no time in unveiling the twist that will raise the stakes to new heights. Classic All Stars rules are back in full effect but with a tantalizing addition. Like in previous seasons, RuPaul will handpick one top all-star who will face off against a secret lip-sync assassin. The victor of this thrilling battle will then hold the power to determine the fate of her fellow queens.

Recently, RuPaul hinted at a brand new twist, promising a competition that is “extra special” and presenting the queens with “two ways to win.” As the queens and viewers alike are left to ponder the possibilities, the excitement then reaches a fever pitch. The anticipation of what lies ahead fuels the fire within each queen, further propelling them to push their boundaries, showcase their talents, and leave an indelible mark on the Drag Race stage.
