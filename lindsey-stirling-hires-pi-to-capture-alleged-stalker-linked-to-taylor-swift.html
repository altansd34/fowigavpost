Lindsey Stirling Hires PI to Capture Alleged Stalker Linked to Taylor Swift

Lindsey Stirling is determined to get rid of her alleged stalker. The violin virtuoso has hired a private investigator (PI) to chase and capture an “obsessed man” who’s apparently followed pop megastar Taylor Swift before.

Lindsey Stirling Asked Private Investigator to Track Her Stalker

Celebrity stalking issues have been a disturbing fact in the Hollywood arena for quite some time now, and it’s unfortunately affecting the lives of the victims negatively. Stirling is apparently not spared from the stalking, and the scenario is reportedly putting her in a devastating position.

In a report published by TMZ, the seasoned musician claimed that the stalker has been “making her life a living hell.” Thus, her immediate need to have the man captured.

Apparently, the stalker is not only interested in Stirling. The man has reportedly been engrossed with Taylor Swift too, for more than a year.

Stirling then hired a PI named Joe DeLu to solve the issue. In a mind-boggling encounter, the PI will confront the stalker to showcase how he irons out stalking incidents.

It might be a question why Stirling decided to employ a PI instead of the police. According to DeLu, there are several law enforcement agencies that elude in sending stay-away orders to stalkers due to fear of being attacked and harmed. Consequently, few to no people would want to address stalkers. This is where PIs enter the picture.

DeLu’s face-off with the alleged stalker will play on a new series on FOX titled TMZ Investigates: Obsessed and Dangerous: Hollywood’s Stalker Crisis. The program will air on Mondays at 9/8c.

Taylor Swift’s Alleged Stalker Arrested After Lurking in Her NYC Home

On Saturday, a man was arrested for allegedly for trying to break into Swift’s New York City apartment in Tribeca. As per a spokesperson for the New York Police Department, a “disorderly person” was reported on Franklin Street.

The police hasn’t confirmed the identity of the man, but a witness revealed that the suspect attempting to “open a door to a building at the location.” The witness also noted that the culprit did not resist arrest.

“It was all pretty civil. He didn’t resist. There was no yelling or anything,” the individual explained. “I first saw him around 1 p.m. — he went up to Taylor’s door. I’m not sure if he knocked or rang the doorbell.”

Meanwhile, Swift’s neighbor stated that the stalker also arrived at Swift’s house before Christmas, noting that her husband even confronted him.

“My husband asked what he was doing here, and he said, ‘I want to see Taylor,'” the source revealed. “He even had flowers at one point.”

The “Lover” singer was seen at Highmark Stadium in Buffalo, New York Sunday for the Kansas City Chiefs and her boyfriend Travis Kelce’s game against the Buffalo Bills.
