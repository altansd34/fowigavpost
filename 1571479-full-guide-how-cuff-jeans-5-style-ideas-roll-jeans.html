Full guide on how to cuff jeans

Cuffed jeans assist in highlighting your footwear while channelling both classic and current trends. What is the proper way to cuff jeans? Here are the best style ideas to roll your jeans.

1. The single cuff

This style is the most basic and casual cuff. It is a classic option that is versatile. Follow the steps below to make a single cuff.

  • Grab the hem of your trousers and fold it upward so that the underside is visible, around one to two inches.
  • Ensure the cuff is uniformly crisp throughout by smoothing the newly added edge.
  • The interior denim weave and the opposing stitch should now be visible, subtly contrasting the exterior hue.
