Sharon Stone Shares Rare Family Snaps to Celebrate Two Important Milestones

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Celebrities often guard their personal lives with utmost privacy, making any glimpses into their private life all the more precious. Sharon Stone, the iconic Hollywood star, recently delighted her followers by sharing rare family photos on social media. These heartfelt snapshots celebrated two special occasions and offered a glimpse into Stone’s family life, showcasing the love and joy that defines her relationship with her loved ones, especially with her three beautiful sons.

Stone’s son Roan turned 23 on June 4th and the actress honored him by sharing two special images. She affectionately referred to Roan as her “beautiful son” and highlighted how good 23 looks on him. The photos captured grown-up-looking Roan in a cozy living room, holding their beloved pet dog.

Becoming a mom was not an easy road for Sharon.

After suffering two miscarriages and trying to have kids with her ex-husband, Sharon finally became a mom in 2000 through the adoption of her first son, Roan.

However, happiness was cut short by a difficult divorce and fierce custody battle that started in 2004. On top of that, Stone faced some serious health problems and barely pulled through.

Stone desperately fought for her son, but she lost primary custody of Roan in 2008. However, despite all of those challenges, Roan and his mom share a very close, loving bond.

The actress later adopted sons Laird in 2005 and Quinn in 2006 and today, the 65-year-old Basic Instinct star is a proud mother of three. “Motherhood didn’t come easily, but it came lovingly to me by angels. I’m grateful my kids chose me, I am a lucky mom,” Stone once confessed.

Adoption was a ’’miracle’’ for Stone.

She admits that adoption forever changed her perspective for the better: “When you adopt, you [realize] any child could be your child, any person could be your relative. After that, you never see the world in the same way again. I’m connected to everyone on this planet. And that’s a miracle in and of itself,” Sharon opened up earlier.

These days Stone radiates joy and happiness.

In May, the Stone family celebrated another joyous occasion as Sharon’s mother reached a significant milestone: her 90th birthday. Stone captured the cheerful spirit of the birthday party through heartwarming snapshots.

She also shared a sweet photo of her mom on Mother’s Day, honoring her with words: ’’To the woman who gave it all to me.’’ In another post, Sharon credited her mom and other close family members for helping her raise her 3 sons.

Stone seems more content than ever.

Scrolling through Sharon’s Instagram photos, we couldn’t help but notice she seems happier than ever. Although she’s been absent for Hollywood for a while and carefully chooses new movie roles these days, she exudes peace and contentment.

She’s enjoying motherhood to the fullest and taking on new hobbies and exploring new talents other than acting. Stone is trying herself as a painter and her art helps her express herself creatively and ’’find herself’’ again.
