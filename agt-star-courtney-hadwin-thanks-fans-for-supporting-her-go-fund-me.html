America’s Got Talent Season 13 finalist Courtney Hadwin took to Twitter earlier this week to thank fans for supporting her Go Fund Me campaign. Hadwin is raising money to travel to the United States so she can record an album.

Fans Support Courtney Hadwin’s Go Fund Me Campaign

Hadwin created her Go Fund Me campaign last October. She shared that for five years, people she worked with in the music industry “prevented me from doing what I love.” However, the 19-year-old is now “completely independent.”

The English singer has released a few singles, and she wants to travel to the United States to work on “an album full of original songs that sound just like the band in my head, done exactly the way I want to hear them.”

“Despite what you might see on the internet, I’m not worth millions of dollars or else I would definitely pay for this myself!” Hadwin shared. “The truth is that I do need help and I appreciate anyone who is interested in being a part of this with me.”

Hadwin Thanks Fans for Their Support

Hadwin has a goal of £100,000, and as of this writing she’s reached £52,516 from over 700 donations. Earlier this week, the singer took to Twitter to thank fans for their support and update them about her career.

On Monday, Hadwin will be performing at Carnegie Hall in New York. After that, she’ll be finishing work on the album. The singer told fans that they “have been so supportive and very patient and I just can’t wait for you to hear the finished product.”

On Friday, Hadwin posted on her Go Fund Me that if she can reach £55,000 by Monday, she’ll release a snippet of a demo for “an upbeat new song” her fans have not heard yet. She also wants to reach £65,000 before she returns from the United States.
