Whats the Real Prize of Winning 'The Masked Singer'? Heres What Fans Think

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Masked Singer

CR: Michael Becker / FOX

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Since the beginning of The Masked Singer, the show has managed to capture fans with its challenging celebrity guessing game. It’s no secret that fans love the thrilling and exciting feeling seconds before each contestant gets revealed. As fans play detective, they also wonder about what the season winner gets. Aside from the trophy, what does The Masked Singer winner actually get?

The Masked Singer Doesn’t Hand Over Cash Prize

During the season, the show doesn’t explicitly announce it’s winning prize. Many long-term viewers expect that the winner receives a cash prize after their victory, but this isn’t the exact case. The Masked Singer winner is given a golden trophy of a mask to celebrate their achievement. The winner also isn’t given any type of cash donation to a charity of their choosing as well.

Nonetheless, contestants are given an appearance fee for their participation on the show. Basically, they are paid per week for the episodes they perform in and obtain a signing bonus. The fee of each celebrity varies depending on their level in the industry. 

In addition to winner prizes, The Masked Singer also used to give awards to its judges. The judge with the most correct first impression guesses wins a Golden Ear trophy. The same award was only introduced in season four and was apparently dropped in seasons seven and eight.

Fans Believe Contestants Join to Reclaim Fame

The prize system clearly isn’t as straightforward as many of the other competition shows we know and love. Thus, this is another moment where fans have attempted to make sense of The Masked Singer. According to a fan on Reddit, celebrities join the show either to launch a potential career in singing or to return their name to the spotlight.

“If our guesses are correct (and they seem to be 100% so far), any of the people who win would be someone who either A) isn’t known for singing and could use this as a chance to ‘launch’ their singing career, or B) hasn’t been mainstream for a while and this is their comeback,” the fan wrote.

In the discussion, another user pitched in and agreed to what the previous user stated. As written in the comments, celebrities appear on the show if they have a project to promote during the year.

Be that as it may, some fans are convinced that personalities compete on The Masked Singer to gain relevance again. But, going into it, they should know that there’s no certain and definite consequence upon a celebrity’s victory on the series. Nonetheless, even without a cash prize, it has to be accepted that The Masked Singer is a successful reality competition show that fans love to watch.
