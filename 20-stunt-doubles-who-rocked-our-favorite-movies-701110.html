20Stunt Doubles Who Rocked Our Favorite Movies

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Modern movies are becoming more and more spectacular. This means that stunt doubles have more work to do. And if you think that only a few actors need stunt doubles, then you are wrong. Even strong guys like Mike Tyson have one. And being a stunt double is a very important job, but many of them don’t get the recognition they deserve.

We at Bright Side hope to fix this situation and give the brave stunt doubles a chance to become famous.
