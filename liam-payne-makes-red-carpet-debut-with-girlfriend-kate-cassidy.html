Liam Payne Makes Red Carpet Debut With Girlfriend Kate Cassidy

On Monday night, former One Direction member Liam Payne and his new girlfriend, Kate Cassidy, made their red-carpet debut. The event was held at the 2022 British Fashion Awards at London’s Royal Albert Hall.

Liam Payne Shows Off With His New Girlfriend on The Red Carpet

Payne showed up to The 2022 British Fashion Awards wearing a black suit to match his new girlfriend, influencer Kate Cassidy. She stunned in a racy black dress with midriff panel cuts and a thigh-high slit.

Although this relationship is not new, it’s the first time the new couple has walked a red carpet together. It was first revealed that the singer was dating the British social media influencer after they attended a Halloween party in a couple’s costume. The party took place at the popular Latin American venue Inca, and the couple attended dressed as Pamela Anderson and her ex-husband Tommy Lee.

It’s said that the music icon and Cassidy saw a sleazy burlesque show right before exiting the popular venue in the early hours. Despite the fact that their relationship is still in its early stages, one insider said that it is very serious.

The couple hooked up nearly six months after Payne and his ex-fiancée Maya Henry called off their engagement. It’s believed that Payne was single after breaking things off with Henry and he met Cassidy soon after.

Inside Liam Payne’s £1000 a Night Hotel in Rome

Payne also recently treated his new lover to romantic weekend in Rome, where they reportedly stayed at a hotel costing £1000 per night. The couple definitely stayed in luxury at their reserved a room at Hotel De La Ville, in the heart of the Italian metropolis. The extravagant rooms have marble bathrooms with large bathtubs and separate walk-in showers, as well as built-in state-of-the-art speakers.

“Liam and Kate are really serious right now. It’s all very new but also very exciting for them both.” A source said. “Liam’s had somewhat of a rocky year but he seems in a much better place right now.”

The pair initially made their blossoming romance public last October, and things are still going well in their relationship. After their red carpet debut, it’s clear that the couple is very serious.
