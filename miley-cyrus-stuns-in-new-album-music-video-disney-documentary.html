Miley Cyrus is truly back and better than ever. After she soared to the top of the charts with “Flowers,” she has finally released her highly anticipated album Endless Summer Vacation. On top of the album’s release, she shared a brand new music video for “River.”

Endless Summer Vacation May Become Another Chart Topper Alongside “Flowers”

Miley Cyrus may skyrocket to the top of the music charts once again with her newest release, Endless Summer Vacation. The insanely talent vocalist released a brand new 13 track album on Friday, March 10.

This album is filled with various highs and lows. It begins with the dynamic hit song “Flowers,” then progresses into even more emotional tunes “Rose Colored Lenses,” “You,” and “Handstand.” The album finishes off with a stripped down version of “Flowers.” Endless Summer Vacation is truly a must listen to album, for anyone. Cyrus’s lyrics are beautiful, emotional, and extremely well written.

Along with the album’s arrival, Cyrus shared a music video and a music documentary on Disney+. Within the documentary, Cyrus will perform seven tracks from her album (including “Flowers”) as well as her hit “The Climb.”

The interview and performance portion of this documentary takes place at the iconic Los Angeles home, seen in the “Flowers” music video. Miley Cyrus Endless Summer Vacation (Backyard Sessions) began streaming on Disney+ on March 10 at 1 p.m. ET.

Did She Film The “River” Music Video in The “Flowers” House?

Ahead of this music video release, there was tons of chatter about the location where it was filmed. Reportedly, the house that “Flowers” and “River” were filmed in was where her ex-husband had allegedly cheated on her with 14 different women multiple times.

This rumor seems to have been debunked with the music video release. If she is in the house, it isn’t noticeable. Throughout the majority of the black-and-white music video, Cyrus is in, what looks like a studio. There’s also seemingly always a white backdrop behind her.

Nonetheless, the superstar is clearly thriving in this video. She’s sporting a breathtaking one-shoulder bodycon dress with strappy heels. The dark color combination, mixed with her bright blonde hair is absolutely stunning.
