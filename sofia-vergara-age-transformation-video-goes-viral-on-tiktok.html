America’s Got Talent judge Sofia Vergara is recently drawing attention on TikTok with her age transformation video. Clearly, fans are in awe with how Vergara’s face and body have minimally changed throughout the years.

Fans are Going Crazy Over Sofia Vergara’s Age Transformation

In a TikTok created by user Retrobx, the short video consists of Vergara’s different eras from when she was 23 years old until present. The video showed a young and attractive Colombian beauty transitioning to a more mature and exquisite woman in her 50s. 

Having seen the video, fans can’t help but be amazed by Vergara’s “vampire” genes. One person stated that the Hot Pursuit actress is “aging like a fine wine.”

“She was gorgeous when she was younger still is. Can’t believe she’s in her 50s,” one user wrote.

“She actually looks better now than she did when she was younger. It’s like the clock for her is reversing. Incredible,” another person commented.

Vergara’s career began when a photographer approached her on a beach in Colombia for a Pepsi commercial. She spent the following years in modeling, appearing on telenovelas, and hosting a travel show. Her break officially kicked off in 2020 when she became a part of Modern Family as Gloria Delgado-Pritchett. During this time, Forbes hailed Vergara as the highest-paid female actress in the world with an annual income of $43 million.

The Modern Family Star Bears No Drama Amid Joe Manganiello Split

Despite her recent split from Joe Manganiello, a source told that Vergara “is doing excellent.” The Magic Mike actor filed for divorce after both parties announced that they were officially calling it quits via a joint statement.

“She is sad that her marriage didn’t work out, but she feels very fortunate anyway,” the source said. “She has a great life that she loves. She is staying at her house for now. Joe is living elsewhere.”

According to the source, there is no drama between the two. Theories regarding the exes’ split ensued when fans noticed that Vergara celebrated her 51st birthday without Manganiello.

The actress also wasn’t wearing her wedding ring at the time. Subsequently, a source revealed that the former couple had just been putting on a “good front publicly” but had already been going through “ups and downs for a long time.”
