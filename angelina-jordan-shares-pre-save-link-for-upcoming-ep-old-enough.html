Angelina Jordan Shares Pre-Save Link For Upcoming EP 'Old Enough'

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Angelina Jordan, the Norwegian singer-songwriter and America’s Got Talent: The Champions star has been captivating audiences with her remarkable vocal abilities and soulful performances since her childhood. Now at the age of 17, she is ready to unveil her latest creation to the world: her highly anticipated EP, Old Enough, and urges fans to pre-save it now.

Angelina Jordan Urges Fans To Pre-Save Her Upcoming EP

Building up excitement on social media, Jordan has been teasing her EP for weeks, leaving fans eagerly awaiting its release. In a recent post, she treated her followers to a glimpse of one of the songs titled “Now I’m the Fool.” This emotional ballad about heartbreak showcases Jordan’s incredible vocal range, leaving listeners astounded.

Old Enough is a labor of love, reflecting Jordan’s personal growth and coming-of-age journey. The EP delves into themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. Jordan’s raw and emotive vocals, coupled with beautifully crafted lyrics and production, make this collection of songs a captivating experience.

In addition to the enticing snippet of “Now I’m the Fool,” Jordan has shared a few other tantalizing teasers from the EP. In one post, she revealed a photo of herself in the studio with the caption, “Getting ready to record some new music.” Another post features a video clip of her singing a snippet from a new song.

While Jordan hasn’t announced the exact release date for Old Enough, she has confirmed that it will be available this summer. Stay tuned for further updates and more information about her highly anticipated EP if you’re a fan of Angelina Jordan.

In the meantime, you can pre-save Old Enough by following the link in Jordan’s bio. Additionally, for an exclusive sneak peek at the EP, be sure to check out the teasers she has shared on her social media platforms.

Old Enough Will Showcase Angelina Jordan’s Growth as an Artist

In the midst of the anticipation surrounding Jordan’s upcoming EP, Old Enough, fans, and music enthusiasts alike are buzzing with excitement. Jordan’s remarkable vocal abilities and soulful performances have garnered her a dedicated following, eagerly awaiting the release of this new musical offering.

With each teaser and snippet shared by the young Norwegian singer-songwriter, the anticipation continues to grow, leaving fans eager to delve into the emotional depths of her latest creation. Old Enough is not only a reflection of Jordan’s personal growth and coming-of-age journey, but it also represents a milestone in her artistic career.

At just 17 years old, Jordan has already demonstrated maturity and depth beyond her years, captivating listeners with her raw and emotive vocals. As she prepares to unveil this collection of songs that delve into themes of love, loss, and self-discovery, it is clear that Jordan’s artistry is evolving, promising a musical experience that will resonate deeply with her audience.
